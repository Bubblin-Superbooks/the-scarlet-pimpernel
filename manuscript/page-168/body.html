Suzanne, and the Vicomte lay concealed among the turnips and cabbages. They, themselves, of course, never suspected who their driver was. He drove them right through a line of soldiery and a yelling mob, who were screaming, 'A bas les aristos!' But the market cart got through along with some others, and the Scarlet Pimpernel, in shawl, petticoat and hood, yelled 'A bas les aristos!' louder than anybody. Faith!” added the young man, as his eyes glowed with enthusiasm for the beloved leader, “that man's a marvel! His cheek is preposterous, I vow!—and that's what carries him through.”
